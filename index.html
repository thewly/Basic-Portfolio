About Me

My professional journey is unconventional, but grounded in practical, on-the-job experience. I am a native Texan who's also lived & worked in Idaho, Utah, California, Alabama & Georgia. Professionally I have worked in some aspect of design for 7 years in the Dallas, TX area. With an English degree, my first job out of university was as a how-to copywriter for a children's face paint manufacturer.

This led to laying out their books & eventually designing their blister cards, brochures, tooling & trade-show booth signage. 2 years ago I moved to an almost purely digital role working for an online lingerie retailer designing their digital marketing content. I worked my way up from production artist to senior graphic designer & along the way added Adobe certification in Photoshop & After Effects as well as becoming a Certified Digital Marketer through the American Marketing Association.

It was with this employer I began working closely with developers & became interested in learning how to implement the prototypes & webpages that I was designing. My boss recommended a program offered through SMU, which is eventually the option I chose.
